================================================================================
  HONEST LIMITATIONS
================================================================================

Inverse Design:
⚠️ GP predictions have ±0.2 eV uncertainty → may miss target
⚠️ Stability is heuristic (tolerance factor only, ignores decomposition)
⚠️ Cost is approximate (raw materials only, no synthesis cost)

Techno-Economics:
⚠️ Material costs are 2026 bulk estimates (vary by supplier/region)
⚠️ No economies of scale (lab-scale, not GW-scale factory)
⚠️ Missing: R&D, IP, QC failures, certification costs

Risk Assessment:
⚠️ TRL is heuristic (pattern matching, not expert review)
⚠️ Supply risk is 2026 snapshot (geopolitics change)
⚠️ Toxicity scoring is simplified (Pb mass fraction proxy)

Recommendation:
✅ Use V6 for SCREENING and RELATIVE COMPARISONS
❌ Not for absolute cost predictions or final decisions
✅ Validate top candidates experimentally before scaling
